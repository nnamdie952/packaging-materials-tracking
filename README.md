# Packaging Materials Tracking

A sustainable packaging system built on Stacks blockchain for material sourcing, environmental impact monitoring, and recycling coordination.

## Features

• **Material Registration** - Register packaging materials with sustainability attributes and carbon footprint data
• **Supplier Management** - Track suppliers with sustainability ratings and certification levels
• **Batch Tracking** - Monitor material batches from production to disposal with environmental impact scores
• **Recycling Coordination** - Record recycling activities with recovery rates and facility information

## Smart Contract

The `packaging-tracker` contract provides comprehensive packaging materials management including material registration, supplier oversight, batch lifecycle tracking, and recycling coordination for sustainable packaging operations.
